Beyond Horizons Care

Where support begins with lived experience.
Support should feel human.

Beyond Horizons Care provides disability, youth, mental health and community support services across Victoria and Queensland. We work alongside children, young people, adults, families, carers and professionals to deliver support that is respectful, culturally aware and built around real life.

Whether you're navigating disability, mental health challenges, youth services, child safety systems or major life transitions, our focus remains the same: to provide support that feels safe, consistent and genuinely person centered.

Why We Exist

Support isn’t just about services. It’s about people trying to navigate complex systems.
Families carrying enormous responsibility, young people searching for stability and individuals wanting to be understood rather than managed.

Beyond Horizons Care was created because we believe support should be more than appointments, paperwork, and funding categories.

Read More

Who We Support

We support children, young people, adults, families, carers and communities across a range of support environments.
From disablilty and mental health support to youth services, child safety, community services and justice pathways.

Our services support people living with:

  1. Physical disability
  2. Psychological disability
  3. Autism and neurodivergence
  4. Intellectual disability
  5. Mental health challenges
  6. Complex and high-support needs
  7. Development and early childhood needs.

We also work alongside people navigating:

  1. Youth services
  2. Child safety services
  3. Community services
  4. Justice and correlational pathways
  5. Major life transitions
  6. Mental health challenges
  7. Social isolation and community circumstances

What Makes Us Different

People First. We see people, not diagnoses, referrals, or funding plans.

Lived Experience Informed. Our understanding comes from real world experience and genuine exposure to the challenges people face.

Cultural Respect: We recognize that culture, faith, language, identity, and community matter.

Safety & Accountability: Strong support requires strong foundations. We take safeguarding, professionalism, and accountability seriously.

Real Relationships: Trust is built through consistency, communication, and showing up when it matters.
