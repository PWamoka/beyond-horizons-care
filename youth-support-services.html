Youth Support Services

Supporting young people with care, structure, and respect

Beyond Horizons Care provides youth support services for young people who need steady, respectful support to navigate everyday life, build independence, and stay connected to education, community, and positive pathways.

We understand that young people often sit at the intersection of many systems — family, school, health, disability, and community services. Our role is to provide consistent, personalised support that feels safe, calm, and reliable.

Our approach to child safety

Beyond Horizons Care provides support in a way that is

Protective

preventing harm before it occurs

Respectful

Honouring children’s voices, boundaries, and dignity

Accountable

Clear processes, reporting, and oversight

Trauma-aware

Understanding behaviour, communication, and emotional safety

Collaborative

Working with families, schools, and services

All support involving children and young people is delivered with appropriate boundaries, supervision, and care.

Safeguarding children and young people

Beyond Horizons Care maintains clear child-safe practices, including.

Zero tolerance for abuse, neglect, or exploitation

Mandatory reporting obligations in line with state legislation

Clear incident management and escalation processes

Safe recruitment, screening, and onboarding of staff

Ongoing training in child safety and protective practices

Supervision and monitoring of service delivery

We ensure all workers understand their responsibilities and act in the best interests of the child at all times.

Listening to children and families

At Beyond Horizons Care, we believe that children and young people have the right to be heard.

We encourage

  1. Open communication with children, young people, and families
  2. Respect for personal boundaries and consent
  3. Raising concerns or questions without fear or judgement

Any concerns raised are taken seriously and addressed promptly and appropriately.

Working with families and professionals

We work respectfully alongside:

Parents and carers

Schools and education providers

Child safety and youth services

Health and mental health professionals

Community and support services

Our role is to provide safe, consistent support while respecting the responsibilities of each system involved.

Raising a concern

If you have concerns about the safety or wellbeing of a child or young person receiving support from Beyond Horizons Care, we encourage you to contact us directly.

We are committed to responding:

Calmly

Respectfully

Promptly

In line with legal and safeguarding obligations

Our responsibility

Child safety is everyone’s responsibility. At Beyond Horizons Care, we are committed to creating environments where children and young people feel safe, respected, and supported always.
